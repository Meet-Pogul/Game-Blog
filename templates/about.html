About Us

 

Welcome to the MSP Gaming Community!

 We are a multi-game community currently serving PC gamers across various titles.  IGC is a group of semi-hardcore players who have gathered together under a single banner to game together and build lasting friendships!

Each of us has a passion for gaming and we are always looking to expand our ranks across all our groups.

Our community is home to a variety of games from MMORPGs to Tabletop RPGs! As you can see above we support several games, each with a 50% or better unique playerbase.  So while there are members part of many groups, you can count on there being a dedicated group for each game.

We are always looking to host new games to build and maintain solid teams and friendships which will make their mark on the gaming world.  To achieve this we are always on the hunt for talented players as well as talented leaders!  If that's you, check us out on our Discord server and we can talk!
